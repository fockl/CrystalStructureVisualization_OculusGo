## Crystal Structure Visualization for Oculus Go

Unity + C#

## How to use:

On Unity:
  The cross key:
    Rotate the direction you're watching
  Space + the cross key:
    Rotate a crystal structure

On Oculus Go:
  Press the front half of the touchpad:
    Go toward the direction you're watching
  Press the back half of the touchpad:
    Go backward

  Drag trigger:
    Rotate a crystal structure
